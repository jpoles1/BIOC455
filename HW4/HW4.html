Question 2

The QSSA dictates that:

\[ \dot{x_2} = x^2 k_x - x_2 k_{-x} = 0 \\ \dot{U_y} = B_y k_{-bx} - U_y x_2 k_{bx} = 0 \\ \dot{{B2}_x} = {B1}_x y k_{by} - {B2}_x k_{-by} = 0 \\ \dot{{B1}_x} = {B2}_x k_{-by} - {B1}_x y k_{by} + U_x y k_{by} - {B1}_x k_{-by} = 0 \]

I can manipulate algebraicly as follows for transcription of y:

\[ U_y = B_y \frac{k_{-bx}}{x_2 k_{bx}}; \quad B_y = D_y - U_y; \quad U_y = (D_y - U_y) \frac{k_{-bx}}{x_2 k_{bx}} \\ U_y + U_y \frac{k_{-bx}}{x_2 k_{bx}} = U_y(1+\frac{k_{-bx}}{x_2 k_{bx}}) = D_y \frac{k_{-bx}}{x_2 k_{bx}} \\ c_x = \sqrt{\frac{k_{-bx}k_{-x}}{k_{bx}k_{x}}}; \quad U_y = \frac{D_y}{(x/c_x)^2 + 1} \]

Likewise for transcription of x:

\[ {B2}_x = {B1}_x y \frac{k_{by}}{k_{-by}} \\ {B2}_x k_{-by} + U_x y k_{by} = {B1}_x k_{-by} + {B1}_x y k_{by} \\ = {B1}_x(k_{-by} + y k_{by}) \\ c_y=\frac{k_{-by}}{k_{by}}; \quad {B1}_x = \frac{{B2}_x k_{-by} + U_x y k_{by}}{k_{-by} + y k_{by}} = \frac{D_x (y/c_y)}{(\frac{y}{c_y}+1)^2} \\ {B2}_x = \frac{D_x (y/c_y)}{(y/c_y)+1} y \frac{k_{by}}{k_{-by}} = \frac{D_x (y/c_y)}{(\frac{y}{c_y}+1)^2} \frac{y}{c_y} \]

Therefore I can derive:

\[ \dot{y} = \frac{dy}{dt} = U_y \beta_y - \gamma_y y = \frac{D_y \beta_y}{(x/c_x)^2 + 1} - \gamma_y y \\ \dot{x} = \frac{dx}{dt} = B2_x \beta_x - \gamma_x x - \gamma_{x_2} x_2 = \frac{D_x\beta_x (y/c_y)^2}{(\frac{y}{c_y}+1)^2} - \gamma_x x - \gamma_{x_2} x_2 \]
