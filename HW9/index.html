Part 1

I begin by deriving the fixed point of the ODE representation of this system:

\[ \dot{x} = \beta - \gamma x = 0 \;\therefore\; x_s = \frac{\beta}{\gamma} \]

I then describe the dynamics of small perturbations about this point:

\[ x_\epsilon = x_s + \epsilon \\ \dot{x}_\epsilon = \frac{d}{dt} (x_s + \epsilon) = \dot{\epsilon} = \beta - \gamma (x_s + \epsilon) \\ \]
